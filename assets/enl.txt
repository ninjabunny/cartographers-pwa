Cartographers
In this game you try to collect as many points as possible over 4 seasons through tasks.
Tasks
Each of the 4 scoring cards gives you points for different conditions that you fulfill.
Tasks
At the end of each season 2 of the 4 scoring cards are evaluated.
Explore the landscape
To complete these tasks you can explore landscapes. By chance a landscape is drawn.
Landscapes
An exploration has a type and a form. You can choose one of them. With this card you can choose between forest or field.
Place
Choose a type and then place this landscape form on the map by selecting the desired location and marking the fields to place.
Confirm
Once you have the shape and its type in your desired position, click on the checkmark.
Great!!
You have explored your first landscape. Now a new one is revealed, which you can place on the map as well.
Explore a landscape
In this exploration you have the choice between two landscape forms. Choose one and place it cleverly to get closer to the scoring objectives.
Season
Every discovery of a landscape takes time, which you can see on the top left of the drawn card.
Season
If the time available to you in a season has been exceeded by all your discoveries, this time ends and a rating is given.
